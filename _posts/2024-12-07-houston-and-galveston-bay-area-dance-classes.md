## [Dance Vision Studio – Clear Lake] | 💰💰💰💰💰

For me, each private lesson was more than \$100. However, the membership allows
you to attend group classes for free. If you take advantage of every single free
group class, maybe the average price per session could be less than \$40.

Private lessons are done via appointment, and group lessons are spread out
regularly throughout the week.

I would recommend these guys if you are wealthy, or have a very important dance
coming up, like a wedding dance.

## [Fred Astaire] | 💰💰💰💰💰

I did not ask the price because the place looked very classy when I walked in,
but I predict it is more than \$100 per private lesson. If you take advantage of
every single free group class, maybe the average price per session could be less
than \$50. They seem to offer fewer group classes than [Dance Vision Studio –
Clear Lake].

I would recommend these guys if you are wealthy, or have a very important dance
coming up, like a wedding dance.
