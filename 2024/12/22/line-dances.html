Shaboozey - A Bar Song (Tipsy)

You begin with your weight on your left foot. In the first 6 steps you are basically making a square. It is not shown in this video, but there is a dance brake that consists of a right tap and a left tap at the end of the sequence before restarting the sequence. After wall 10 you have to make a step touch (4 steps).

  1. right foot to the right
  2. tap
  3. right foot forward
  4. tap
  5. left foot to the left
  6. tap
  7. left foot backwards
  8. tap
  9. step right
  10. tap
  11. step left
  12. tap
  13. step right with right foot
  14. back cross step to the right with left foot
  15. step right with right foot
  16. tap
  17. step left
  18. tap
  19. step right
  20. tap
  21. step left with left foot
  22. back cross step to the left with right foot
  23. step left with left foot
  24. kick with right foot while doing 1/4th turn to the left
  25. right foot heel
  26. right foot tap
  27. left foot heel
  28. left foot tap
  29. jump while opening feet to shoulder width
  30. hold
  31. right hip
  32. left hip

Pitbull ft. John Ryan - Fireball

You begin with your weight on your left foot and on the toes of your right foot, as though you were tapping the floor. You are doing 4 cross steps moving forward.

  1. 3 steps forward (right, left, right) and kick with your left leg
  2. 3 steps backward (left, right, left) and tap with your right foot (now you should be in the starting position)
  3. 3 steps forward (right, left, right) and kick with your left leg
  4. 3 steps backward (left, right, left) and tap with your right foot to the right (this will make it easier to cross your feet in the next step)
  5. left cross and tap
  6. right cross and tap
  7. left cross and tap
  8. right cross and tap
  9. jazz step while doing a half clockwise turn (right foot crosses left foot, left foot steps back, right foot returns to neutral position)
  10. jazz step (but no turn)

Laura Bell Bundy - Two Step ft. Colt Ford

This one is a 16-count. You begin standing straight on both feet.

  1. slide right
  2. bring feet together
  3. slide right
  4. bring feet together and do a triple step
  5. slide left
  6. bring feet together
  7. slide left
  8. bring feet together and do a triple step
  9. kick with you right foot
  10. bring feet together and do a triple step (kick stomp stomp, AKA kick ball change)
  11. kick with you right foot
  12. bring feet together and do a triple step (kick stomp stomp, AKA kick ball change)
  13. slide right while doing a 1/4th of a quarter turn to the left
  14. slide left while doing a 1/4th of a quarter turn to the left
  15. slide right while doing a 1/4th of a quarter turn to the left
  16. slide left while doing a 1/4th of a quarter turn to the left

Pitbull, Kesha - Timber

You begin with your weight on your left foot and on the toes of your right foot, as though you were tapping the floor, because the first step is a forward step.
Notice that the first 8 steps are essentially 4 rock steps. Then you do 4 triple steps forward beginning with your right foot.

  1. right foot steps forward
  2. left foot steps in place
  3. right foot steps backwards
  4. left foot steps in place
  5. right foot steps forward
  6. left foot steps in place
  7. right foot steps backwards
  8. left foot steps in place
  9. right foot steps forward and does a triple step
  10. left foot steps forward and does a triple step
  11. right foot steps forward and does a triple step
  12. left foot steps forward and does a triple step
  13. 8 steps back (left, right, left, right, left, right, left, right) back to starting position, with your weight on both feet
  14. right hip
  15. left hip
  16. do a pivot step to do half a counter-clockwise turn (your weight is on your left foot and you turn on the ball of your left foot as you take 2 or 4 small steps with your left foot to turn you)
