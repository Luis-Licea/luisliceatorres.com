A pattern that Ray _Trusty_ taught me.

- Start in a {{% a "righty" %}} position.
- On 1, step back while turning 90 degrees counter-clockwise.
- On 2, turn 90 degrees counter-clockwise so the follower is facing your back. As you perform the half turn, bring your right hand over your right shoulder and then drop the follower's hand on your shoulder.
- On 3&4 turn 180 degrees counter-clockwise such that the followers hand slides around the back of your neck and down your left arm, leaving you in an {{% a "open" %}} position.
