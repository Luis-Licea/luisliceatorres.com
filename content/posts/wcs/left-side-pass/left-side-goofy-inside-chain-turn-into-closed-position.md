A pattern that [Bob Bowlby] and Joan Peeples taught me.

- Prep 1 is on line with the follower's shoulder.
- Prep 2 is on line with the follower's face.
- Step 3&4 is over and behind the followers head such that the follower's hand
  gets released on step 4 so your right hand can slide down the follower's head.
- On step 4 step back into the slot into closed position.
