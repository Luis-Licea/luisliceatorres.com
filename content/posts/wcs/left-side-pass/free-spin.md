A [goofy] unconnected [right-side inside chain turn].

- Start from a [goofy] position. The footwork is the same as in a left-side
  pass.
- On step 2 prep to the left and on step 3 throw the hand to the right so the
  follower turns counter-clockwise.
- On 3, back drop so that your backwards body movement causes the follower to
  rotate counter-clockwise.
- On 4 don't walk into the slot if possible; step in place.
- On 5, catch the follower's hand.
- On 5&6 do an anchor step. You should end in a [open] position.
