- Start from an [open] position. The footwork can be the same as in a [left-side
pass]. However, it looks better if on step 4 your body faces 9 o'clock because
the follower is also facing 9 o'clock. 
- On 5&6 anchor step to face 6
o'clock while the follower faces 12 o'clock. 
- End in an [open]
position.

As for the handwork:
- on beats 1 thru 3 do a [left] hand prep at waist level. 
- On 3&4 use you left-hand middle and ring fingers and a sign-of-horn for the handle
on beats. You may also do a bracelet around the follower's wrist using your
pinky and thumb as she rotates on 3&4.
