- The leader's left hand ring finger and middle finger point to the right, holding the follower's right hand (pointing down) at xiphoid level.
- The leader's right hand ring and middle finger point to the left, holding the follower's left hand (pointing down) at xiphoid level sort of like the characters |=|.
