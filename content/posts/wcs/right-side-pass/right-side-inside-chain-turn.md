---
title: "right-side inside chain turn"
date: 2026-06-15T01:31:02-05:00
description: ""
categories: Dance
tags: West Coast Swing
---

- Start from an {{% a "open" %}} position. This is a right-side pass with chain 2.5 turns instead of 1.5 turns.
- On 1 step back and diagonally backward.
- On 1.5 give a [left] prep toward her right ear and immediately begin spinning her counter-clockwise for 2.5 turns. If you give two preps instead of one, then the turning will begin on step 3, and by that time it will be too late.
- On 2 begin the spinning.
- End in a {{% a "open" %}} position.
