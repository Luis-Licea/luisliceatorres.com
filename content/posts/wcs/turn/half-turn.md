A 180 degree traveling turn that happens in 2 beats.

- Begin facing 6:00 while anchoring on the left foot.
- On 1, step towards 6:00 with the right foot pointing at 7:30.
- On 2, step toward 6:00 with the left foot and use the momentum to rotate clockwise about the right foot.
- End with with the left foot pointing at 11:00 and the right foot pointing at 12:00.

This is the footwork that the follower should do a the beginning of a whip.
