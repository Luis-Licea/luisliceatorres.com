- Start in an [open] {{% a "righty" %}} position.
- Start facing 9:00 while the follower faces 6:00.
- On 1, step towards 6:00 with the left foot.
- On 1.5, [left prep] to turn the follower counterclockwise 0.75 turns to go from facing 6:00 to facing 9:00
- On 2, step towards 12:00 with the right foot, standing split weight (this makes the pattern close)
- On 2, both should face 9:00 and she should be to your left
- On 2, change the [righty] handhold to a [left] handhold by grabbing the over the palm, not under the palm, and not the wrist
- On 3, lean towards 12:00 over right foot to wind up
- On 4, step on the left foot and begin to turn counterclockwise
- On 5, your bend right arm should go over your left arm as you face the follower
- On 5.5, release [left] handhold and trap the follower's right hand under your right armpit as you rotate counterclockwise
- On 5.5, raise your left arm straight up to the ceiling so it can land across the follower's shoulder and back
- On 6, place your left arm across the follower's back
- Walk around
- End in an [open] [left] handhold.
