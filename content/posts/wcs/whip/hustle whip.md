- The footwork is the same as in a whip.
- For the handwork, a [parallel] handhold is necessary.
- Open the arms on 1,2 and close them by 3&.
- Open the hands on 4,5, and close them by 6.
- Post on 6 for the anchor step.

You may do a {{% a "car-wash whip" %}} exit.
