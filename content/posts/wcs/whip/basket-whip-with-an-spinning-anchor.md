- It looks like a basket whip with an accelerated tuck turn exit.
- It is the same as a [basket whip] up to step 4.
- On step 5 raise the left hand on and release the right hand, turning the follower from step 6 thru 8 with plenty of energy so she can do a double spin. Make sure to draw a halo around her head twice.
