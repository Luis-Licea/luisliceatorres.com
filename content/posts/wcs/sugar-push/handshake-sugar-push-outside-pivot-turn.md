- Start in a [righty] or {{% a "righty-up" %}} position.
- On step 2 raise your right hand to her face level and align your right hand a couple of inches above her forehead while your hand points at 10 o'clock.
- On step 3&4 do compression on 3& and begin the turn on 4 as you step forwards.
- Anchor on 5&6.
- End in a [righty] position.
