- Start from an {{% a "open" %}} or [goofy] position.
- The footwork and handwork are the same as in a [sugar push] up to 4.
- The footwork on 5&6 is the same as in a salsa left turn.
- During 5&6 switch the follower's left hand from your right hand onto your left hand.
- End in a {{% a "lefty" %}} position.
