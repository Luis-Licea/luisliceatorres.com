- Same as a {{% a "head drape" %}}, but the hand goes over the leader's head. Also known as a head-wrap.

- Start in a {{% a "righty" %}} position; a {{% a "lefty" %}} hold also works. The footwork is the same as in a sugar push; only the handwork changes. For the head-wrap, bring your right and her right hand to your nose level on 1 while stepping back.
- On 2, move your right hand from your nose level to your nape and leave her right hand on your nape.
- On 3&4 she may slide her right hand down your left arm to go into a [goofy] handhold; post at the end of 4 to avoid moving further down the slot.
- On 5&6 do an anchor step.
- End in a [goofy] position.

If you start in a {{% a "lefty" %}} position, the {{% a "head drape" %}} would end in an {{% a "open" %}} position.
