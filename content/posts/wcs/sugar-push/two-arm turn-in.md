Same as {{% a "single-arm turn-in" %}}, but use both hands to do a {{% a "left hammerlock" %}}. It is technically possible to achieve the same by doing a sugar tuck while holding the follower's left hand, but the correct way is to do a [pump] prep, which happens at waist level. You should end in a {{% a "left hammerlock" %}} position.
