The footwork is the same as in a sugar push for the leader. Use it from an {{% a "open" %}} hold. Steps 1 and 2 are for executing a [piston] so the follower will turn 180 degrees into [back-to-front] position by the end of step 2. On 3& raise your left arm over her head. On 4 gently push her back in between her shoulder blades so she knows to walk under the arm. Steps 5&6 are an anchor step. You should end in a [back-to-front] position.
