Same as a {{% a "hairbrush" %}}, but the hand goes over the follower's head. Also known as a head-wrap.

- Start in a {{% a "righty" %}} position; a {{% a "lefty" %}} hold also works.
- The footwork is the same as in a sugar push; only the handwork changes. For the head-wrap, bring your right and her right hand to her nose level on 1 while stepping back.
- On 2, move your right hand from her nose level to her nape and leave her right hand on her nape.
- On 3&4 slide your right hand down her left arm to go into a {{% a "right" %}} handhold and post to avoid moving further down the slot.
- On 5&6 do an anchor step. You should end in a {{% a "right" %}} position.

If you start in a {{% a "lefty" %}} position, the {{% a "head drape" %}} would end in an {{% a "open" %}} position.
