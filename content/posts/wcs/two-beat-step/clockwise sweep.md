A rond de jambe.

Instead of doing the anchor step for a duration of 2 beats, slide your right foot from 12, to 3, to 6 o'clock in a round motion.

Pattern suggestion:

- On a {{% a "right-side inside turn" %}}, replace steps 5&6 with a sweep.
