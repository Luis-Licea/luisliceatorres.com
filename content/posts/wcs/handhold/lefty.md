Leader left to follower left handhold.

This is a mirror image of the [righty] position because it requires a left-to-left hand connection instead of a right-to-right hand connection, sort of like the characters |\\|.

Reached via:

- {{% a "behind-the-back whip" %}}

Examples:

- A double handed sugar tuck into a hammerlock.
- A double handed sugar push inside turn into a sweetheart.
- A crossed-up sugar push inside turn into a cross-down handhold.[^1]
- A crossed-down sugar push outside turn (sugar tuck) into a cross-up handhold.[^1]
- A double handed sugar tuck with a left hand release into a {{% a "right" %}} handhold.[^1]

[^1]: This is a hypothetical move. I have not actually tried it. :smile_cat:
